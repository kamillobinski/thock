## [What's New](https://github.com/kamillobinski/thock/compare/1.18.0...1.18.1)

> Bug fixes and more control over your audio setup.

### New Features

- **Volume Control in Settings**
  There's a sleek new volume slider chilling right in your sound settings.

- **Audio Device Selection**
  Headphones on, headphones off, speakers blasting - whatever your vibe, you can now swap your audio output device directly in the settings.

- **Quick Soundpack Access**
  There's now a handy-dandy button in settings that zaps you straight to the custom soundpack directory. Drop 'em in and get thocking.

### Improvements

- **Settings Layout Refinement**
  The 'About' stuff is now cozying up with the 'General' tab.

### Bug Fixes

- **Fixed Audio System Stability**
  Resolved critical memory leaks and timer lifecycle bugs that could cause audio to stop working after extended use. Improved thread safety for idle timeout management and fixed pitch variation frame tracking issues.
