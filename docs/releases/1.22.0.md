### New Features

- **Keyboard Cleaning Mode**
  A new Utilities section in Settings lets you block all keyboard input while keeping your mouse and trackpad fully functional. Perfect for wiping down your keys without accidentally launching apps, typing into windows, or triggering shortcuts. Assign a shortcut to toggle it on and off without reaching for the mouse.
